Our story

A dream years in the making

Fitness has been our passion for over ten years. What we love most isn't just helping people change physically. It's helping them discover the confidence, strength, faith, and resilience they already have inside them.

After more than a decade working in other gyms, we knew we wanted to build something different right here in Tom Bean, Texas: a place where people across Grayson County feel seen, supported, and inspired. We believe fitness is for everyone, no matter your age, your experience level, or your starting point.

We want this to feel like home.

Our hope is that every person who walks through our doors experiences peace, encouragement, belonging, and community. More than a place to work out. A place to be made new.

Good questions

Frequently asked

How much is membership?+
Individual is $45/month, Couples is $80/month, Family is $115/month (family of 3) or $135/month (family of 4), and our Military, First Responder & Student rate is $40/month (valid ID required). Add tanning for $20/month, sauna for $10/month, or both for $25/month. Just visiting? A day pass is $15 and a 7-day pass is $30. All memberships are month-to-month. See the full breakdown above.
Do I need experience to start?+
Not at all. Zero experience required. You don't have to know what you're doing yet. We'll help you start, and you'll never be judged for being a beginner.
Will someone show me how to use the equipment?+
Yes. We'll walk you through the space and the equipment so you feel confident. Want a deeper plan? Personal training is there whenever you're ready.
Do you offer personal training and coaching?+
Yes, both personal training and nutrition & wellness coaching. It's the clearest path to structure, accountability, and lasting transformation.
Is there a kids area?+
Yes. We have a kids room with a couch, TV, and snack area so parents can work out while their kids have a safe space to relax.
What amenities are included?+
Free weights, machines, cardio, stretch & recovery space, sauna, and tanning, plus supplements, premade meals, and drinks on site. Red light therapy is coming soon.
Where is The Gym located?+
We're in Tom Bean, Texas (Grayson County), easy to reach from Sherman, Denison, Van Alstyne, Whitewright, Bells, and Howe. Send us a message and we'll share the exact address and directions.
Are you open now?+
Yes! The Gym is open in Tom Bean. You can sign up online in just a few minutes, then stop by to grab your key fob and get started.
Can I tour before I join?+
Absolutely. Bring your questions and we'll show you around. Request a tour below and we'll be in touch.
